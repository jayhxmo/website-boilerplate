InFuture

Hackathon for the Innovators of Tomorrow

May 28th - 29th · New York City

More Than Just a Hackathon

Our mission is to create an open environment on May 28th - 29th for an awesome, full 2-day hackathon. We truly believe that the high school hacking community deserves an awesome event to connect and get inspired. Hackers are what make a hackathon amazing, and we believe that by bringing the best hackers and giving them the best experience possible, we can have a really amazing event with a long lasting impact.

At InFutue, it's all about the hackers

What is InFuture?

InFuture is a hackathon that will gather 200 of the nation's most motivated and innovative high schoolers for an amazing event in the spring of 2016.

Why are we doing this?

High school students deserve a way to express their ideas in a professional environment, as college students and adults often have. We believe high schoolers can innovate the future.

Who can apply?

Every high school student is welcome to apply! We also accept applications from middle school / homeschooled students, provided your age is in the appropriate ballpark. If you're unsure if you can apply, shoot us an email.

When will applications open?

While we won't be giving a definitive date on when applications will open, anyone who subscribes to our email list by entering their email above will receive a notification the moment applications open.

Can I apply as/with a team?

We ask that every potential attendee apply individually to allow us to get a better sense of who will be attending the event. However, if you get accepted and a team member does not, shoot us an email and we'll try our best to sort it out.

What are you looking for?

While showing us previous projects you've worked on, other hackathons you've attended, etc. are all important, we are looking for motivation and passion above anything else. If you're not very experienced, don't fret! Everyone has their own strengths, and we'd love to hear yours.

When and where?

InFuture will be taking place on May 28-29 at Rise NY (43 W. 23rd Street NYC). Check-in will open at 10:00 AM on Saturday, and the doors will close at 8:00 PM on Sunday.

How much will it cost?

Nothing! Everything is free, that's the beauty of hackathons. We do, however, recommend you bring money to use public transportation and for emergencies.

How can I get there?

In order to provide hackers from further areas greater opportunity to attend InFuture, we ask that anyone in the general Tri-State area drive or use public transit to get here. Luckily for you hackers from outside the Tri-State area, we will allow you to apply for reimbursements on a case-by-case basis.

How big can my team be?

Teams have a maximum size of 4 hackers. You don't need to be in a team in order to make and submit a hack.

What should I bring?

All you need to bring is your laptop and charger, any devices you want to hack on, and school ID. We also recommend you bring money for public transit, a sleeping bag, and a change of clothes.

I have another question!

We would love to answer you question. Please email us at team@infuture.io for any additional information you need.
